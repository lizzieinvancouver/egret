## Some info on these files
* They are described broadly in: https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2020.02.21.960161v1 but I also emailed with Barnabas about them who told me:

> Sorry that you were unable to access the species-level maps and I have not gone back to look at the files since the paper’s publication in 2021. I however, have a much updated versions of the maps used in a companion study and they are available locally on my computer and I can share via Dropbox or physically with a flash drive in Florida. The maps are available as vector polygons in geopackage format, for over 200k species.

I am fairly sure the 'companion study' is this paper: https://www.pnas.org/doi/abs/10.1073/pnas.2319989121 but we should confirm how Barnabas would like us to cite these maps.
